Welcome to MET

Our mission is to make your people connections easy.

Many of us who attend networking events often forget content of the conversation. What if you can look back at your conversations? Current NLPs do not function well in noisy environments. However, MET will be able to differentiate from other NLPs by collating recordings from various devices and utilizing proximity of devices.
