Welcome to MET

Our mission is to make your people connections easy.

MET is an app that uses NLP and LLM in networking events.

It’s a social media app marketed towards networking event and exhibition organisers. It lets the organisers to:

  1. Track connections made by participants
  2. Give a report on the participants
    • Summary of topics of discussion
    • Monitoring Groups
  3. Assists participants
    • Instantly notify the name and profile of the person/people you’re talking to
    • Scribe that's functional in noisy environments
    • Transcript and Summarisation of your conversation

Many of us who attend networking events often forget content of the conversation. What if you can look back at your conversations? Current NLPs do not function well in noisy environments. However, MET will be able to differentiate from other NLPs by collating recordings from various devices and utilizing proximity of devices.
